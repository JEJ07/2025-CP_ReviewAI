Overview

ReviewAI is a Chrome extension that analyzes product reviews on supported e-commerce websites (such as Amazon, Shopee, Lazada, Walmart, eBay, and others) to detect potentially fake reviews using AI.

Data Collection

  • ReviewAI does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, passwords, or financial data.
  • ReviewAI does not collect health, location, or web history data.
  • The extension reads only public product reviews displayed on the websites you visit.
  • Any review data accessed is used solely for AI-powered analysis and is not stored or shared.

Remote Code

ReviewAI may fetch AI analysis scripts and model data from our secure server at https://www.reviewai.live to perform real-time review analysis. No personal data is transmitted to our server; only public review content is processed.

Data Use

Data accessed is used exclusively to provide insights on the authenticity of product reviews. ReviewAI does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

Security

All communication with our server uses HTTPS. No user credentials, financial info, or personal identifiers are stored or transmitted.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our extension or legal requirements. The updated policy will be available at this page.
