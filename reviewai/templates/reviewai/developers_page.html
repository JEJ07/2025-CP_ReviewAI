About Our Capstone Project

ReviewAI represents our final year project, combining theoretical knowledge from our BSIT curriculum with practical application to address the growing problem of fake reviews in e-commerce.

Problem Statement

Fake reviews mislead consumers and harm business credibility. Our goal is to develop an AI system that can accurately identify suspicious review patterns and linguistic markers.

Our Approach

We use machine learning techniques such as Natural Language Processing (NLP), sentiment analysis, Support Vector Machine (SVM), Random Forest, and the deep learning model DistilBERT to build a comprehensive fake review detection system.
